The Growth of D&D
September 13, 2020

Beginnings & Businesses. Emerging from the minds of Gary Gygax and Dave Arneson, D&D was published by TSR (Tactical Studies Rules) alongside Don Kaye in 1973. In 1990 the company was purchased by Magic the Gathering creator Wizards of the Coast, as it remains today. In 1999 Wizards of the Coast was acquired by Hasbro, providing a greater distribution of miniatures and further promoting this collaborative dice-rolling-hobby. D&D ultimately discovered success, but its journey wasn’t without darker days.
